Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

At NFC Labs, accessible from www.nfclabs.com, we use cookies to enhance your experience while using our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookie usage.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve user experience, and provide analytical data to the website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be turned off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions you make, such as logging in or filling in forms.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit the most. They help us improve our website and provide a better experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language preferences) and provide personalized features to improve your experience.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to track your browsing habits and show you relevant advertisements based on your interests.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Make our website work properly
  • Improve website performance
  • Enhance user experience by remembering your preferences
  • Understand how users interact with our website to improve our services
  • Show relevant advertisements based on your browsing behavior

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also allow third-party cookies, such as analytics providers and advertisers, to place cookies on your device. These third-party cookies are governed by the privacy policies of the third parties, and not by this Cookie Policy.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. You can also delete cookies stored on your device at any time.

If you choose to disable cookies, please note that some parts of the website may not function properly.

6. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as outlined in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, please manage your cookie preferences or stop using our website.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
