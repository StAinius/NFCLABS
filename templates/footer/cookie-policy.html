Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to improve your experience

Last updated: May 5, 2025

At NFC Labs, we believe in transparency. This policy explains how we use cookies on our website and your choices regarding their use.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

Essential for website functionality - cannot be disabled.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how you use our website to improve it.

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and personalize your experience.

Targeting Cookies

Show you relevant advertisements based on your interests.

How We Use Cookies

✓ Make our website work properly
✓ Improve website performance
✓ Remember your preferences
✓ Analyze website usage
✓ Show relevant content

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties (like analytics providers) to place cookies on your device. These are governed by their own privacy policies.

Managing Your Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Some features may not work if cookies are disabled

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our cookie usage as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent by adjusting your browser settings.

Policy Updates
